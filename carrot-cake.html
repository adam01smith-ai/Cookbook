🥕 Carrot Cake
🍰 Dessert 🥕 Bake 🧀 Iced

Servings: 10

Moist spiced carrot cake with cream cheese icing and optional nuts.

Ingredients

  • ml vegetable oil
  • g natural yoghurt
  • eggs
  • 1½ tsp vanilla extract
  • zest of ½ orange
  • g self-raising flour
  • g light muscovado sugar
  • tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ nutmeg (finely grated)
  • g carrots (grated)
  • g raisins
  • g walnuts (optional)
  • Icing:
  • g butter
  • g icing sugar
  • g soft cheese

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan).
  2. Oil and line two 20cm cake tins.
  3. Whisk oil, yoghurt, eggs, vanilla and orange zest.
  4. Mix flour, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and a pinch of salt.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  6. Stir in carrots, raisins and half the nuts.
  7. Divide mixture between tins.
  8. Bake for 25–30 minutes until a skewer comes out clean.
  9. Cool in tins.
  10. Beat butter and sugar until smooth.
  11. Add soft cheese gradually and mix until creamy.
  12. Sandwich cakes with half the icing.
  13. Spread remaining icing on top and finish with nuts.

Add the soft cheese gradually to stop the icing splitting.

Tips

  • Use freshly grated carrots for moisture
  • Don’t overbake — keeps cake soft
  • Best eaten at room temperature
