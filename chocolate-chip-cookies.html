🍪 Chocolate Chip Cookies
🍰 Dessert 🍪 Bake 🍫 Sweet

Servings: 12

Soft in the middle, slightly crisp on the edges, loaded with chocolate chunks.

Ingredients

  • g butter
  • g soft brown / caster sugar
  • egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • g plain flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • g chocolate chips
  • ¼ tsp salt

Method

  1. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Beat in egg and vanilla.
  3. Fold in flour, baking powder, chocolate and salt.
  4. Mix gently — do not overwork the dough.
  5. Chill dough overnight for best flavour (optional but recommended).
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan).
  7. Roll into rough balls and space on baking trays.
  8. Bake:
    • Fresh dough: 8–10 minutes
    • Chilled dough: 10–12 minutes
  9. Bake until golden at edges but soft in the centre.
  10. Cool on tray briefly before transferring.

Cookies will firm up as they cool — don’t overbake.

Tips

  • Chilling improves flavour and texture
  • Rough dough = better looking cookies
  • Slightly underbake for soft centres
