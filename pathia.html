🍛 Pathia Curry

Servings: 2

Sweet, sour and spicy tomato-based curry.

Ingredients

  • ml oil
  • cassia bark
  • bay leaf (optional)
  • g onion (finely chopped)
  • tsp ginger/garlic paste
  • tsp chilli powder
  • tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • tsp mix powder
  • tsp smoked paprika
  • tsp salt
  • tbsp tomato paste
  • tsp kasuri methi
  • tbsp coriander stalks + leaves
  • ml base gravy
  • Main ingredient (chicken, lamb, prawns, etc.)
  • tsp mango chutney
  • tsp brown sugar
  • tsp lemon juice
  • tsp tamarind sauce

Ingredients adapted from the Pathia recipe source. 【1-190094】

Method

  1. Heat oil and fry the whole spices briefly to flavour the oil.
  2. Add onion and cook until soft and lightly browned.
  3. Add ginger/garlic paste and cook until the raw smell goes.
  4. Add the spices and fry briefly, using a little base gravy to stop burning.
  5. Add tomato paste and cook on high heat until the oil separates.
  6. Add your main ingredient and coat in the sauce.
  7. Add base gravy gradually, letting the sauce reduce between additions.
  8. Stir in mango chutney, sugar, tamarind and lemon juice.
  9. Simmer until thick, glossy, and balanced sweet/sour.
