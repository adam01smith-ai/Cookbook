🍲 Base Gravy

Servings: 2

Use this as a base sauce for curries.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • carrot
  • pepper
  • garlic cloves
  • ml water
  • g tomatoes

Method

  1. Cook vegetables gently until soft (no browning).
  2. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  3. Add spices and stir well.
  4. Add tomatoes and water.
  5. Simmer for 20–30 minutes.
  6. Blend until smooth.
