🌶️ Roasted Red Pepper Soup

🥣 Soup 🔥 Roasted 🌿 Flavourful

Servings: 4

Deep, smoky soup made from roasted peppers and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • red peppers, halved & deseeded
  • white onion, halved
  • garlic cloves
  • celery sticks, chopped
  • g plum tomatoes
  • ml vegetable stock
  • tbsp olive oil
  • tbsp tomato purée
  • tbsp sundried tomato paste
  • tsp chilli flakes
  • g butter
  • salt & black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place peppers, onion (cut side down), tomatoes and garlic on a baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Roast for 30 minutes until soft and slightly charred.
  4. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large pan and gently cook celery for 4–5 minutes.
  5. Mix the stock with tomato purée, sundried tomato paste and chilli flakes, then add to the pan.
  6. Peel the roasted onion and garlic, roughly chop, and add them along with the roasted tomatoes.
  7. Blend until smooth using a hand blender.
  8. Gently heat without boiling, then season to taste.
  9. Serve warm, ideally with crusty bread.

Roasting the vegetables gives this soup its deep flavour.
