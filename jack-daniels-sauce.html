🥃 Jack Daniel’s Style BBQ Sauce

🔥 Sticky 🍯 Sweet 🥃 Whiskey

Servings: 2

Sweet, smoky, sticky glaze — perfect for chicken, ribs, burgers or loaded potatoes.

Ingredients

  • ml water
  • ml pineapple juice
  • ml teriyaki sauce
  • ml soy sauce
  • g dark brown sugar
  • g garlic (minced)
  • ml olive oil
  • ml lemon juice
  • g white onion (minced)
  • ml whiskey
  • g crushed pineapple
  • tsp cayenne pepper

Method

  1. Add water, pineapple juice, teriyaki sauce, soy sauce and brown sugar to a saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Reduce heat and let it gently simmer.
  4. Add minced garlic and whisk to combine.
  5. Add olive oil, lemon juice, onion, whiskey, crushed pineapple and cayenne pepper.
  6. Stir everything together.
  7. Simmer for 40–50 minutes until reduced by about half and thick and syrupy.

Don’t let it boil over. Sauce thickens further as it cools.

Use With

  • Grilled chicken
  • Ribs
  • Burgers
  • Loaded potato bowls
