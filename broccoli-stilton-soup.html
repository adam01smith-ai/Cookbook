🥦 Broccoli & Stilton Soup

🥣 Soup 🧀 Creamy 🥦 Healthy

Servings: 4

Creamy soup with broccoli and rich blue cheese flavour.

Ingredients

  • tbsp rapeseed oil
  • onion, finely chopped
  • celery stick, sliced
  • leek, sliced
  • potato, diced
  • 1 knob butter
  • ml vegetable or chicken stock
  • head broccoli, chopped
  • g stilton (or blue cheese)
  • black pepper

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan and cook the onion until soft.
  2. Add celery, leek, potato and butter. Stir until melted, cover and cook gently for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the stock and add the broccoli stalk pieces. Cook for 10–15 minutes until soft.
  4. Add the rest of the broccoli and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Blend until smooth.
  6. Stir in the stilton, leaving a few small chunks if you like.
  7. Season with black pepper and serve.

Don’t over-boil after adding cheese — it can split.
