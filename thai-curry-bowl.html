🥥 Thai Curry Rice Bowl

🌶 Spicy 🥥 Creamy 🍚 Bowl

Servings: 2

Thick, clingy Thai-style curry sauce with rice and toppings.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts (or tofu)
  • tbsp oil
  • tbsp red curry paste
  • ml coconut milk
  • ml stock
  • tbsp fish sauce (or soy)
  • tsp sugar
  • lime
  • pepper
  • carrot
  • g greens (beans/broccoli)
  • tsp cornflour
  • g rice
  • ml water (per portion)

Method

  1. Cook your rice as normal and set aside.
  2. Heat oil and fry chicken until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add curry paste to the pan and fry for 20–30 seconds.
  4. Add coconut milk and let it bubble hard for 1–2 minutes to begin thickening.
  5. Add a small amount of stock and stir.
  6. Add vegetables and return chicken to the pan. Simmer until cooked through.
  7. Mix cornflour with a little water and stir into the sauce to thicken.
  8. Add fish sauce, sugar and lime juice. Taste and adjust.
  9. Simmer briefly until the sauce is thick and coats everything.

The sauce should stick to the meat and not run like a normal curry.

Build Bowl

  1. Rice
  2. Thick curry
  3. Fresh coriander
  4. Chilli + lime
  5. Crunch (peanuts or crispy onions)
