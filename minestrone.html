🍲 Minestrone
🥣 Soup 🇮🇹 Italian 🍝 Hearty

Servings: 4

Classic Italian vegetable soup with beans, pasta and greens.

Ingredients

  • tbsp olive oil
  • onion
  • celery stick
  • carrot
  • courgette
  • g pancetta
  • garlic clove
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • g cannellini beans
  • g chopped tomatoes
  • tbsp tomato purée
  • ml vegetable stock
  • 1 bay leaf
  • g small pasta
  • g greens
  • basil (to serve)
  • parmesan (to serve)

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan.
  2. Add onion, celery, carrot, courgette and pancetta.
  3. Cook gently for 10 minutes until softened.
  4. Stir in garlic and oregano and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add beans, tomatoes, purée, stock and bay leaf.
  6. Season and simmer for 30 minutes.
  7. Add pasta and greens.
  8. Cook for a further 10 minutes.
  9. Serve with basil, parmesan and a drizzle of olive oil.

Add extra stock if the soup thickens too much while cooking.

Tips

  • Use seasonal vegetables where possible
  • Add parmesan rind while simmering for depth
  • Tastes even better the next day
