🍝 Spaghetti Carbonara

Servings: 2

Creamy Roman pasta with pancetta, eggs and cheese (no cream).

Ingredients

  • g pancetta
  • g pecorino cheese
  • g parmesan
  • eggs
  • g spaghetti
  • garlic cloves
  • g butter
  • salt & black pepper

Method

  1. Bring a large pan of salted water to a boil.
  2. Finely chop pancetta and grate cheeses. Mix cheeses together.
  3. Beat eggs with black pepper and set aside.
  4. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until al dente.
  5. Lightly crush garlic cloves.
  6. Melt butter in a pan, then cook pancetta with garlic until golden.
  7. Remove and discard garlic.
  8. Add cooked spaghetti to the pancetta pan, keeping some pasta water.
  9. Mix most of the cheese into the eggs.
  10. Remove pan from heat, then add egg mixture and mix quickly.
  11. Add a little pasta water to loosen into a creamy sauce.
  12. Season and serve with remaining cheese.

The heat from the pasta cooks the eggs — do not overcook or it will scramble.
