🍗 Creamy Garlic Chicken

Servings: 2

Quick creamy chicken — ready in about 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • tbsp olive oil
  • knob butter
  • cloves garlic
  • ml double cream
  • ml white wine
  • tsp Dijon mustard
  • lemon (juice)
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Method

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook chicken for 5–6 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Remove and rest.
  4. Add garlic to the pan and cook for about 30 seconds.
  5. Pour in white wine and let it reduce for 1 minute.
  6. Add cream and Dijon mustard, then simmer 2–3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  7. Add lemon juice and adjust seasoning.
  8. Return chicken to the pan and coat in the sauce.

Serve With

  • Buttered new potatoes
  • Green beans or asparagus
