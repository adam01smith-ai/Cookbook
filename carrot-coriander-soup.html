🥕 Carrot & Coriander Soup

🥣 Soup 🧡 Healthy 🌿 Fresh

Servings: 4

Smooth, warming soup with sweet carrots and fresh coriander.

Ingredients

  • tbsp vegetable oil
  • onion, chopped
  • tsp ground coriander
  • potato, chopped
  • g carrots, peeled & chopped
  • ml vegetable or chicken stock
  • handful fresh coriander
  • salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and cook the onion for about 5 minutes until softened.
  2. Add ground coriander and potato, then cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add carrots and stock. Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat.
  4. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes until the carrots are tender.
  5. Blend with the fresh coriander until smooth (in batches if needed).
  6. Return to the pan, season to taste and reheat gently before serving.

Blend in batches carefully — hot soup expands in a blender.
