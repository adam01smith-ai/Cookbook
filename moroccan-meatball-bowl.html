Rice rule: 75g rice + 90ml water per person

🥩 Meatballs

  • g mince
  • onion (grated)
  • garlic cloves
  • tsp cumin
  • tsp coriander
  • tsp smoked paprika
  • tsp cinnamon
  • egg
  • Salt & pepper, parsley (to taste)

Method

  1. Mix, roll small meatballs.
  2. Brown in a pan with a little oil; remove.
  3. They finish cooking in the sauce.

🍅 Thick Sauce

  • g chopped tomatoes
  • g tomato purée
  • tsp cumin
  • tsp paprika
  • ml honey
  • Optional: harissa to taste

Method

  1. Toast spices 30 seconds.
  2. Add tomatoes + purée + honey; season.
  3. Simmer 5–8 min until thick.
  4. Add meatballs; simmer 10–15 min.

🍚 Japanese Rice (hob)

  1. Rinse rice 3–4 times until water is mostly clear.
  2. Add the calculated water.
  3. Boil uncovered, then lid on low heat 10–12 min (don’t lift lid).
  4. Rest 10 min (lid on), then fluff.

🍽 Build the bowl

  1. Rice
  2. Meatballs + sauce
  3. Yogurt (lemon + salt)
  4. Cucumber/tomato/onion + herbs
  5. Crunch (nuts) + chilli oil/harissa
