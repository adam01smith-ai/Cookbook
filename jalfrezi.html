🍛 Jalfrezi Curry

Spicy High heat Uses Base Gravy

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • ml oil (for scorching onion & pepper)
  • ml oil (for the curry) — shown as a scaled range
  • g onion (½ medium-large)

  • green pepper
  • tsp ginger/garlic paste
  • tsp mix powder
  • tsp chilli powder
  • tsp garam masala
  • tsp tandoori masala
  • tsp salt
  • tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek)
  • fresh green chillies (sliced lengthways)
  • tbsp coriander stalks (chopped)
  • tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • ml base gravy (heated)
  • tbsp tomato paste
  • Main ingredient (e.g., pre-cooked chicken, lamb, veg)

Method

  1. Cut the onion half into chunky segments and separate the layers. Cut the peppers into chunky pieces. 【1-732ee5】
  2. Heat a small amount of oil in a very hot pan, then quickly fry the onion and peppers until the outside is browned and smoky. Set aside. 【1-732ee5】
  3. Add the larger amount of oil to the same pan. Cook the ginger/garlic paste briefly until it begins to colour. 【1-732ee5】
  4. Stir in the spices (kasuri methi, chilli powder, mix powder, garam masala, tandoori masala) and salt. Fry briefly, loosening with a splash of base gravy so it doesn’t burn. 【1-732ee5】
  5. Add the tomato paste and coriander stalks, then add your main ingredient and cook on high heat until the oil rises. 【1-732ee5】
  6. Add base gravy in stages, letting the sauce reduce and thicken between additions. 【1-732ee5】
  7. Return the scorched onion and peppers to the pan, add the sliced green chillies and tomato quarters, and heat through to finish. 【1-732ee5】
