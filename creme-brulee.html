🔥 Crème Brûlée
🍰 Dessert 🍮 Classic 🔥 Torch

Servings: 4

Classic creamy custard with a crisp caramelised sugar topping.

Ingredients

  • ml double cream
  • ml whole milk
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • egg yolks
  • g caster sugar
  • extra sugar for topping

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan).
  2. Place ramekins in a deep roasting tin.
  3. Heat cream and milk in a pan.
  4. Split vanilla pod and scrape seeds into the cream.
  5. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale.
  6. Heat cream until just below boiling.
  7. Slowly pour hot cream into eggs, whisking.
  8. Strain mixture through a sieve.
  9. Remove foam from the surface.
  10. Pour mixture into ramekins.
  11. Add hot water to the tray (bain-marie).
  12. Bake 30–35 minutes until just set with a slight wobble.
  13. Cool slightly, then chill in fridge until fully set.
  14. Sprinkle sugar evenly over the top.
  15. Use a blowtorch to caramelise until crisp.

The centre should wobble slightly — overbaking makes it firm.

Tips

  • Use a bain-marie for even cooking
  • Chill overnight for best texture
  • Caramelise just before serving
