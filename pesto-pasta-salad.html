🌿 Pesto Pasta Salad

❄️ Cold 🌿 Fresh ⚡ Quick

Servings: 2

Fresh, herby pasta salad with pesto instead of dressing.

Ingredients

  • g pasta
  • tbsp pesto
  • g cherry tomatoes
  • cucumber
  • red onion
  • g feta or mozzarella
  • olive oil (optional)
  • salt & black pepper

Method

  1. Cook pasta until just done (al dente).
  2. Drain and rinse under cold water to cool.
  3. Chop vegetables and add to a large bowl.
  4. Add cooled pasta and pesto.
  5. Mix well until everything is coated.
  6. Add cheese, season, and mix again.

Add a splash of olive oil if it feels dry.

Extras

  • Cooked chicken
  • Olives
  • Spinach or rocket
  • Toasted pine nuts
