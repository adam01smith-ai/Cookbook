🥄 Basic Vinaigrette

🥗 Dressing ⚡ 5 mins 📏 Ratio

Servings: 2

Simple oil + acid dressing you can use on any salad.

Ingredients

  • tbsp olive oil
  • tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
  • tsp Dijon mustard
  • tsp honey
  • salt & black pepper

Classic formula: 3 parts oil : 1 part acid

Method

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl or jar.
  2. Whisk or shake until combined.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  4. Use immediately or store in fridge.

Shake again before using if it separates.

Variations

  • Balsamic: use balsamic vinegar, reduce honey
  • Lemon: swap vinegar for lemon juice
  • Garlic: add 1 minced clove
  • Herb: parsley, basil or oregano
