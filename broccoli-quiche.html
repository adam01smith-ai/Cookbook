🥦 Broccoli Quiche

🥧 Pastry 🧀 Cheesy 🥦 Healthy

Servings: 6

Easy quiche using store-bought pastry with broccoli and cheese.

Ingredients

  • ready-made pastry case (or rolled pastry)
  • head broccoli, chopped
  • onion, finely chopped
  • eggs
  • ml milk or cream
  • g cheddar cheese, grated
  • tbsp butter or oil
  • salt & black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. If using raw pastry, line a baking dish and blind bake for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Cook broccoli in boiling water for a few minutes until just tender, then drain well.
  4. Heat butter or oil in a pan and soften the onion.
  5. Whisk together eggs, milk, salt and pepper.
  6. Add broccoli, onion and cheese into the pastry case.
  7. Pour over the egg mixture.
  8. Bake for 35–45 minutes until set and golden.
  9. Cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Let it rest before cutting — helps it set properly like a classic quiche.
