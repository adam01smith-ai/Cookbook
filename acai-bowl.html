🫐 Acai Bowl

Servings: 1

Thick smoothie-style bowl with fruit and crunchy toppings.

Base

  • acai packet (frozen)
  • banana (frozen)
  • g mixed berries (frozen)
  • ml milk (almond/coconut etc.)
  • tsp honey (optional)

Keep the liquid low so it stays thick.v>

Toppings

  • g granola
  • banana (sliced)
  • g fresh berries
  • tbsp chia seeds
  • g coconut flakes

Toppings add crunch, flavour and texture. 【2-8f3e45】

Method

  1. Add frozen acai, banana and berries to a blender.
  2. Add a small amount of milk and blend slowly.
  3. Only add more liquid if needed — keep it thick.
  4. Blend until smooth and scoopable (like soft ice cream).
  5. Pour into a bowl.
  6. Add toppings on top and serve immediately.
