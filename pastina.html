🍲 Creamy Pastina
🍝 Pasta 🥛 Creamy ⚡ Quick

Servings: 2

Simple creamy Italian comfort food made with butter, milk and Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • g pastina
  • ml vegetable stock
  • tbsp butter
  • tbsp milk or cream
  • g Parmesan
  • salt & pepper

Method

  1. Bring stock to a boil.
  2. Add pastina and cook, stirring often.
  3. Cook until soft and most liquid is absorbed.
  4. Turn heat to low.
  5. Stir in butter and milk or cream.
  6. Add Parmesan and mix until creamy.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.

Stir constantly at the end for a smooth, creamy texture.

Tips

  • Add more milk if too thick
  • Use cream for richer texture
  • Stir often to release starch and thicken
