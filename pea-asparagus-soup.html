🥣 Pea & Asparagus Soup
🥣 Soup 🌱 Fresh 🍃 Light

Servings: 8

Smooth, fresh pea and asparagus soup finished with cream and chives.

Ingredients

  • tbsp olive oil
  • onions (finely diced)
  • clove garlic
  • ml chicken stock
  • g peas
  • g asparagus
  • ml double cream
  • salt & black pepper
  • tbsp chives

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic and cook gently for 10 minutes until soft.
  3. Add stock and bring to the boil.
  4. Add peas. When boiling again, add asparagus.
  5. Simmer for 20 minutes until very soft.
  6. Blend until smooth, then strain through a sieve.
  7. Stir in half the cream and season to taste.
  8. Reheat if serving hot, or chill if serving cold.
  9. Mix chives into remaining cream and swirl into soup before serving.

Straining gives an extra smooth, restaurant-quality finish.

Tips

  • Use frozen peas for consistent sweetness
  • Don’t overcook asparagus to keep colour bright
  • Add extra stock for a thinner soup
