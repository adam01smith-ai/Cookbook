🥗 Lemon Chicken Salad

🥗 Fresh 🍋 Zesty ⚡ Quick

Servings: 2

Light but filling salad with a fresh lemon dressing.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • tbsp olive oil
  • salt & black pepper
  • g lettuce / greens
  • cucumber
  • g cherry tomatoes
  • red onion
  • avocado (optional)

Dressing

  • tbsp olive oil
  • tbsp lemon juice
  • tsp honey
  • tsp Dijon mustard
  • clove garlic (optional)
  • salt & pepper

Method

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook in a pan with oil for 5–6 minutes per side until done. Rest and slice.
  3. Mix all dressing ingredients in a bowl or jar.
  4. Combine lettuce, veg and chicken in a bowl.
  5. Pour dressing over and toss lightly.

Add dressing just before serving to keep everything fresh.

Extras

  • Feta cheese
  • Toasted nuts
  • Chilli flakes
